DoingNow is a modern habit and routine companion built for people who want more consistency, structure, 
and clarity in their everyday lives without dealing with bloated productivity systems. The app is designed
around a simple philosophy: meaningful progress comes from small repeated actions performed consistently over time.
Instead of overwhelming users with complex dashboards, aggressive gamification, or endless configuration screens, 
DoingNow focuses on creating a calm and distraction-free experience that helps users stay aligned with their routines
in the most lightweight way possible.

The core experience revolves around building sustainable habits through recurring routines and reliable reminders. 
Users can create habits, customize schedules, choose recurring weekdays and times, and receive notifications that 
help reinforce daily consistency. The experience is intentionally minimal, fast, and focused, allowing users to quickly 
interact with their routines and continue with their day without friction. Every interaction in the app is designed to 
reduce cognitive load while maintaining a clean and modern interface that feels intentional rather than overwhelming.

From a UI and UX perspective, DoingNow emphasizes simplicity, smooth interactions, subtle animations, and a clean 
visual hierarchy that keeps attention on what actually matters. The app is built to feel lightweight and calming 
instead of noisy or hyper-stimulating. Rather than trying to maximize screen time, DoingNow is designed to quietly 
support users in building routines that naturally integrate into their lives. The product direction prioritizes 
reliability, long-term usability, and thoughtful design over feature overload.

A major part of DoingNow is its notification and scheduling system. The app includes timezone-aware recurring reminders, 
exact alarm support on Android, reboot persistence, and notification scheduling designed for long-term reliability. 
The goal is to ensure reminders remain dependable across app restarts, device reboots, timezone changes, and operating 
system restrictions so users can trust the system every day without having to constantly manage it manually.

DoingNow is currently in active development, with ongoing improvements focused on notification reliability, performance, 
scheduling stability, and overall user experience refinement. Planned future features include streak systems, progress 
insights, smarter reminder handling, widgets, deeper routine analytics, cloud synchronization, and additional customization 
options while still preserving the app’s minimal and focused identity.

Early access and updates for DoingNow can be found at:

https://doignow.lovable.app/

The project is being built using Flutter and Dart with a strong focus on performance, maintainability, 
cross-platform consistency, and platform-native behavior. DoingNow is not trying to become the most 
feature-heavy productivity app on the market. Its purpose is to become a reliable everyday system that users 
genuinely return to because it quietly helps them stay consistent over time.
